Social and Cultural ActivitiesFounded school, Junior Degree Colleges/and undertook construction of school buildings in Ongole, Nellore and Prakasham Districts in Andhra Pradesh, particularly in villages; takes keen interest in use of technology for the welfare of mankind; took part in various activities aimed at spread of literacy and political awakening through personal interactions with masses in villages; Construction of Community Halls, Community Centres and Marriage Halls; supply of drinking water through tankers in villages; providing ambulances for health purposes, orgzanising health centres; necessary assistance to the sick and destitute; provided relief and rehabilation to Tsunami victims and of natural disasters like floods, rains etc. in Andhra Pradesh; organising prgrammes and health camps at ward level for the upliftment of the oppressed and depressed sections of society and to secure social justice to them by promoting socio-cultural activities
Special InterestsIdentification of immediate needs and long term requirement of the public in general and getting schemes prepared with the help of technical people through our resources in addition to Govt. Schemes; have been monitoring State Government/Govt. of India Schemes and get them implemented to ensure that the benefit reaches to all section of the people in the Consitutency; arranging State level conferences and meetings relating to Panchayati Raj, Party and leading several delegations to solve and mitigate the problems of the people particularly of Tabacco farmers; taken keen interest in the establishment of airport at Ongole; modernization of Bucklingam Canal flowing through Ongole; laying of new railway lines between Nadikudi and Sri Kalahasti and Ongole-Donakonda and provision of additonal bypass to Ongole Town while 6-laning of NH-5.
