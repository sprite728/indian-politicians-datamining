Social and Cultural ActivitiesThrough his foundation, he provides ambulance service in the desert districts of Barmer and Jaislmer, his home districts. To promote and disseminate the literature of `Dingal`(an ancient local language) he has established another foundation. He is also a trustee on various charities engaged in running hospitals, museums and water conservation projects.
